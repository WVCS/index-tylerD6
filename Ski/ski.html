    About the Lodge

    On your next winter break, consider staying at Westview Ski Resort. With our system of log cabins carefully picked out, you can view the beautiful mountains. Cabins are high quality featuring a fireplace, kitchen, bedroom, living room, and more. Our snow is high quality(not made from snow machine). Our resort also features a variety of different slopes for skiing and snowboarding (There will be lessons only for skiing) We can't forget to mention our latest prototype of snowlifts, that travel faster and more efficient than ever(Waiver required)
    Ski Lessons

    If you are looking for a quality skiing experience, look no further. Our mountains have many different slopes ranging from beginners to experts. If you don't have ski gear, rental ski will always be provided at high quality.
