    • Summer Fishing Event

    Hosted at Lake Miramar
    Requirments:
    Fishing rod
    Fishing license
    Bait is provided
    Must be 16 years or older
    Overview:
    This event is a contest on whoever can catch the biggest fish, determines by weight
    There will be a contest for largemouth bass, bluegills, and trout
    Any other species of fish caught won't qualify in the competition
    Rules:
    You will have 2 hours the respective species of each competition, who ever catches the largest one of the day will receive a prize. Fish are to be released if they don't qualify and you will be disqualified if you kill fish under their legal size
    Rewards
    Bluegill: $1000
    Trout: $2500
    Largemouth Bass: $5000
    Sign Up Online
    Only 20 participants per contest, you aren't allowed to compete in more than one contest unless the event lacks contestants
